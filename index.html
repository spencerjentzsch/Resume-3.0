About Me

I love tech, operations, and people. That intersection is where I am the most happy and the best at my job.

My background is extremely varied—from running a Tinder consultancy to executive coaching and from being a kayaking instructor to being CEO of a fully remote international travel company and trying to stop monkeys from chewing through internet cables. I also used to be a wild bat catcher using the high-tech equipment of a plastic ice cream bucket and a baseball mitt.

With this wide breadth of experiences, I've managed almost every aspect of a company (front-end dev, QA, HR, strategy, PR, finance, sales, marketing, PM, logistics, customer service, copywriting, etc.) over the years.

As you can imagine, I have a lot of fun stories from my various ventures. Hit me up if you want to know more.
